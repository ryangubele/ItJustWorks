# The name and logo

Everything here is MPL-2.0 - fork it, fix it, take it over. That's encouraged, not merely allowed.

The identity is the one thing that doesn't ride along for free. Carry *this* project forward - same mod, new steward - and the name comes with it; that's the torch, and it's yours to pick up. Ship *your own* version instead, and give it its own name and its own face. "It Just Works RELOADED", "It Finally Just Works", "It Barely Just Works", anything you like - just not this name and this logo on a different mod. So a player can tell yours from this one, and a bug in one doesn't land on the other's doorstep.

The ™ is a joke; there's no lawyer behind it. This is a courtesy, and courtesy is usually enough.
